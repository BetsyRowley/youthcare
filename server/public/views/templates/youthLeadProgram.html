The Community Service Stars component of YouthLEAD employs youth part time to address community needs with local nonprofit organizations while developing employment and financial literacy skills.

Program Impact

At the end of the 2014-15 program year, YouthCARE staff asked YouthLEAD participants, "What do you like best about YouthCARE?" Below are some of the responses:

  • "You can be yourself"
  • "Meet new people"
  • "The people are welcoming"
